Market Context

Industrial Market Trends

The Jacksonville industrial market continues to experience strong demand, particularly for modern distribution facilities near transportation infrastructure. Key trends include:

  • Growing e-commerce demand driving need for last-mile distribution centers
  • Increased activity from third-party logistics providers (3PLs)
  • Strong absorption in the 50,000-150,000 sq ft range
  • Limited availability of quality bulk space

Recommendations

Strategic Recommendations

  1. Target value-add opportunities: Look for well-located but functionally obsolete properties that can be renovated for modern uses.
  2. Focus on last-mile locations: Properties within urban infill locations serving local populations are in high demand.
  3. Consider build-to-suit: With limited quality inventory, partnering with users on build-to-suit projects can be attractive.
  4. Explore sale-leasebacks: Many owner-users may be interested in monetizing real estate while maintaining occupancy.
